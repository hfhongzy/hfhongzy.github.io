About

⭐️Hi! I am Hong Zhaoyang, a second-year undergraduate from the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. (GPA: 3.90 / 4)

I am currently conducting research on vector databases in the SUSTech DBGroup under the guidance of Prof. Bo Tang.

I plan to apply for a CS PhD program for admission in Fall 2026. Currently, I am seeking for self-funded research internships for Spring 2025, supported by the finances and policies of SUSTech Turing Class.

I have a keen interest in system design and mechanisms. I also enjoy socializing and collaborating with others. Additionally, I am self-disciplined, balancing studies, life, and physical health well. Most importantly, I also maintain a positive mindset and resilience to stress and failures.
