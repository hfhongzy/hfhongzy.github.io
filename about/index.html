⭐️Hi! I’m Zhaoyang (Cyrus) Hong, a third-year undergraduate at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. Since 2024, I have been a member of the SUSTech DBGroup under the supervision of Prof. Bo Tang. I’m currently a research intern at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Prof. Lin Ma, Nishil, and Barzan. My research interests include novel database systems (e.g., GPU databases) and machine learning systems (MLSys).

I plan to apply for CS PhD programs for Fall 2026. If you’re a professor recruiting PhD students, I’d be excited to connect.

I enjoy socializing and collaborating with others, so feel free to reach out if you’d like to chat!
